Cactus is located in the heart of Milan, inside Gaslite Square. What was once a vacant building, in 2012, Jose decided rennovate the building and convert it into an authentic Mexican restaurant. Jose coming from Puebla, Mexico, he wanted to introduce food originating from his hometown and the mexican culture to the people of Milan, hoping that when they finish eating, they leave happier than when they arrived.
